What exactly is a dynamic QR card? +

A dynamic QR card allows you to change the destination URL or content at any time without having to reprint the physical QR code itself. This provides flexibility for evolving campaigns.

How does pricing and the plans work? +

Plans are accumulative — each purchase adds on top of your existing quota with its own expiry date. For example, buying a plan twice doubles your active quota across those respective billing periods.

Are the generated QR codes for commercial use? +

Yes, all QR codes generated on our platform, regardless of your plan, are fully cleared for personal and commercial use without any restrictions.

What if my printed code gets damaged? +

Our QR codes have up to 30% error correction, meaning they will likely still scan even if partially damaged. However, we highly recommend reprinting if essential elements are unreadable.

Can I design a custom QR with my logo? +

Absolutely! You can add custom colors, choose unique patterns, and insert your brand's unique logo right in the center for a highly personalized look.
